Our Team

San Francisco Digital Services


The San Francisco Digital Services Team is transforming how residents interact with the City. We're doing this by building services designed around the people that use them.

Led by the Chief Digital Services Officer, our team includes:

  • product managers
  • designers
  • service designers
  • user researchers
  • developers
  • content strategists

We believe in four principles:

  1. Put the needs of residents first
  2. Focus on delivery and outcomes over process
  3. Create an agile and data driven culture
  4. Make City services accessible to everyone.
