Equity statement

We are committed to equity in our hiring, team culture, and product outcomes and fostering an inclusive work environment. We are building a team that reflects a diverse set of lived experiences and identities. As a team, we strive to be anti-racist. We commit to being inclusive, accessible, and equitable.
